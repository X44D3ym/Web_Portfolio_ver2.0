PassCSE APP

PassCSE is a comprehensive reviewer app we developed to help users prepare for the Civil Service Exam. Built using Android Studio, it features a range of practice questions in Analytical Ability, Numerical Ability, and Verbal Ability, with randomized questions and a time limit for each assessment. The app offers a smooth user experience, providing instant feedback and tracking progress to help users assess their readiness for the exam.

VPES SIMS (ongoing)

The VPES Student Information Management System (SIMS) is the new, streamlined platform for managing essential academic information. Students can use SIMS to enroll, easily view their grades, and check their class schedules. For our faculty, the system provides a convenient way to view teaching schedules and efficiently upload student grades, ensuring an organized and up-to-date academic record for everyone
